  ---
  Graphic Strategy: 5 Logo Concepts

  ---
  CONCEPT 1: "The Connection Hub" ⚡

  Visual Metaphor: Interconnected nodes, bringing people together

  Rationale: Your core value proposition is connecting guests to events through
  RSVPs and seating arrangements.

  Design Elements:
  - Primary Shape: Interlocking geometric forms or stylized nodes
  - Typography: Clean, modern sans-serif; "KALFA" or abstract monogram
  - Color: #6C4CF1 (brand) as primary, with subtle gradient depth
  - Supporting Elements: Thin connection lines suggesting coordination
  - RTL Friendly: Symmetrical or adaptable to both directions

  Variations:
  1. Minimal: Single icon (no text) - favors versatility
  2. Wordmark: Typographic only - favors brand recognition
  3. Combination: Icon + wordmark - favors clarity

  ---
  CONCEPT 2: "The Smart Grid" 🎯

  Visual Metaphor: Seating arrangements, structured organization

  Rationale: Differentiates your platform from generic event management by
  emphasizing the unique seating feature.

  Design Elements:
  - Primary Shape: Abstract grid/table representation or stylized chair
  arrangement
  - Typography: Bold, compact sans-serif
  - Color: #6C4CF1 with #5A3DE0 (brand-hover) accent
  - Supporting Elements: Clean grid lines or dot patterns suggesting structure
  - RTL Friendly: Balanced grid works in any orientation

  Variations:
  1. Geometric: Stylized grid with brand color fills
  2. Linear: Outline-only grid (favors dark mode versatility)
  3. Dot-matrix: Pattern suggesting connection points

  ---
  CONCEPT 3: "The Digital RSVP" ✉

  Visual Metaphor: Envelope, communication, response

  Rationale: RSVP is the core action - your platform enables this communication
  loop.

  Design Elements:
  - Primary Shape: Stylized envelope or checkmark icon
  - Typography: The brand name in modern, trustworthy lettering
  - Color: #6C4CF1 with Indigo 600 secondary (tech connection)
  - Supporting Elements: Subtle checkmark or "R" letter abstraction
  - RTL Friendly: Envelope shape is naturally adaptable

  Variations:
  1. Badge Style: Circular background with envelope icon
  2. Lettermark: "R" monogram design (recognition)
  3. Responsive: Complex envelope (large) → Simple envelope (small)

  ---
  CONCEPT 4: "The Modular Organizer" 📦

  Visual Metaphor: Modularity, system components, building blocks

  Rationale: Multi-tenant structure suggests flexibility and component-based
  thinking.

  Design Elements:
  - Primary Shape: Stylized hexagon or modular shapes (6-sided = organizations,
  6 = RSVP)
  - Typography: Integrated or standalone, clean sans-serif
  - Color: #6C4CF1 with gray 700 neutral support
  - Supporting Elements: Negative space between modules suggesting flexibility
  - RTL Friendly: Modular shapes work identically in any direction

  Variations:
  1. Connected: Hexagons with subtle connectors
  2. Stacked: Layered modules showing depth
  3. Isometric: 3D perspective suggesting technology

  ---
  CONCEPT 5: "The Modern Minimal" 🎨

  Visual Metaphor: Professional SaaS, clean, trustworthy

  Rationale: Aligns with the professional, technical brand personality without
  being overly literal.

  Design Elements:
  - Primary Shape: Bold letter "K" with modern geometric accents
  - Typography: "KALFA" in modern sans-serif with custom character spacing
  - Color: #6C4CF1 with gradient fade to #A594F9 (brand-light)
  - Supporting Elements: Minimal geometric accents (dot, line, or square)
  - RTL Friendly: Letter-based design requires symmetrical balance

  Variations:
  1. Dark Mode Optimized: High-contrast version for dark backgrounds
  2. Monogram: "K" only - favors small spaces
  3. Typographic: Full wordmark - favors primary usage

  - Font Family: Modern sans-serif (Inter, system-ui-sans, or similar)
  - Weight: Medium (400-500) for wordmarks, Bold (600-700) for headings
  - Character Spacing: Slightly increased for Hebrew readability
  - Alignment: RTL-aware; ensure balance works mirrored

  - ViewBox: 0 0 24 24 (icon) or custom for wordmarks
  - Paths: Clean, optimized curves (no unnecessary nodes)
  - Class Names: fill-current, stroke-current for color inheritance
  - Optimization: Minify for production, keep source for editing
